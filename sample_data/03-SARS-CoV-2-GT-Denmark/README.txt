About the dataset

The dataset was used in the article by Itok et.al shown below. Read
the article to see the background of the analysis and the
interpretation of results.

Ito K, Piantham C, Nishiura H. Estimating relative generation times
and reproduction numbers of Omicron BA.1 and BA.2 with respect to
Delta variant in Denmark. Math Biosci Eng. 2022 Jun
21;19(9):9005-9017. doi: 10.3934/mbe.2022418. PMID: 35942746.

Acknowledgment

The count data of variants were obtained from the metadata of
sequences of SARS-CoV-2 submitted to GISAID from Denmark. 
The information on originating laboratories, submitting
laboratories, and authors of SARS-CoV-2 sequence data were listed in
gisaid_acknowledgment.xlsx.
